About Me

Engineering with purpose & precision

I'm a Mechanical Engineering (Hons.) graduate from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, with a strong foundation in design, simulation and product development. My interests sit at the intersection of thermal-fluid systems, mechanical design and emerging EV technology.

During my internship at VEGA Innovations — Sri Lanka's pioneering EV company — I worked on Li-ion battery packs, laser welding optimisation, heat-sink design and non-destructive testing, sharpening my skills in CAD/CAE and thermal management on real production hardware.

My final-year research on a hydrodynamic tunnel for experimental fluid dynamics earned the IMechE Sri Lanka Final Year Project Award (2025). Alongside engineering, I lead as Chairman of IET On Campus and stay hands-on with programming, IoT and rapid prototyping.
